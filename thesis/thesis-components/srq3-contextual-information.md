# SRQ3: Contextual Information & Decision Support

## Research Question

**To what extent does additional contextual information improve the predictive and decision-support capabilities of AI systems?**

## Scope

This notebook focuses on understanding:
1. **Context types**: Business context, domain knowledge, external signals
2. **Feature engineering**: How to incorporate context into models
3. **Knowledge representation**: Ontologies, semantic structures
4. **Improvement metrics**: How to measure context impact on predictions
5. **Real-world examples**: Cases where context was critical to success

## Paper Selection Criteria

Include papers on:
- Feature engineering and domain knowledge integration
- Knowledge graphs and ontologies in ML
- Transfer learning and domain adaptation
- Contextual bandits and contextual decision-making
- Business intelligence and context in analytics
- Time series with exogenous variables
- Explainability through context
- Few-shot and zero-shot learning (learning from limited context)
- Retrieval-augmented generation (RAG) for context
- Domain-specific models that leverage context

Exclude papers on:
- Pure feature selection without conceptual context discussion
- Theoretical analysis of context without empirical results
- Context in non-decision-support domains

## Key Concepts to Track

When reading papers, look for:
- **Context sources**: Where does context come from?
- **Integration methods**: How is context combined with model inputs?
- **Measurement of impact**: What improvement metrics?
- **Scalability**: How much context is needed? Diminishing returns?
- **Interpretability**: Does context improve explainability?
