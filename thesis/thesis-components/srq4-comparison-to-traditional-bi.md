# SRQ4: Comparison to Traditional Business Intelligence

## Research Question

**How does the proposed predictive AI system compare to traditional descriptive analytics approaches used in business intelligence systems?**

## Scope

This notebook focuses on understanding:
1. **Traditional BI architectures**: OLAP, data warehouses, dashboards
2. **Descriptive vs. predictive analytics**: Differences and complementarities
3. **Implementation patterns**: How organizations use traditional BI
4. **Limitations of traditional approaches**: What problems remain?
5. **Hybrid approaches**: Combining traditional BI with modern AI

## Paper Selection Criteria

Include papers on:
- Business intelligence architectures and data warehouses
- OLAP and multidimensional analysis
- Dashboard and visualization in BI
- Data quality and governance in BI systems
- Limitations of descriptive analytics
- Evolution from BI to AI/ML analytics
- Real-world BI system deployments
- Change management in BI/AI transitions
- Performance comparison studies (traditional vs. modern approaches)

Exclude papers on:
- Pure data visualization without BI context
- Theoretical database design without business relevance
- Non-business analytics (scientific data systems)

## Key Concepts to Track

When reading papers, look for:
- **BI capabilities**: What can traditional BI do well?
- **Limitations**: Where does traditional BI fall short?
- **Predictive gaps**: What predictive problems can't BI solve?
- **Implementation effort**: Cost and complexity of traditional BI?
- **AI advantages**: What do modern AI approaches provide?
- **Integration**: How to combine traditional BI with AI?
