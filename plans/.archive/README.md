# plans/.archive/

This folder holds completed or stale plans that have been archived.

**To archive a plan**: move the entire plan folder here and update its frontmatter `status: cancelled` or keep `status: complete`.

**Naming stays the same**: `P{NNNN}_YYYY-MM-DD_HH-mm_<slug>/`

Legacy plans from the old status-bucket system (P0001–P0022) remain in their original `plans/{01-backlog_plans,...}/` folders and are not migrated here.
