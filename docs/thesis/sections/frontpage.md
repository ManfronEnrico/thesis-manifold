## Notes

- **Confidentiality**: If Nielsen data requires it, add "CONFIDENTIAL — not for public distribution" below the title
- **Character count**: CBS calculates standard pages as total characters (excl. spaces) ÷ 2,275
  - Tool: Word → Review → Word Count → Characters (with spaces)
  - ⚠️ CBS uses characters INCLUDING spaces — do NOT use "characters (no spaces)" count
  - Target: ≤273,000 characters incl. spaces (120 × 2,275) for body text only
- **What counts**: all body text (Ch.1–10 + abstract) — not front page, bibliography, appendices
- **Font/margin**: ≥11pt font, 3cm top/bottom margins, 2cm left/right (CBS requirement)

## Title candidates

| Version | Pros | Cons |
|---|---|---|
| "A Multi-Agent AI Framework for Demand Forecasting in FMCG Retail..." | Precise, covers all SRQs | Long |
| "Augmenting Retail Demand Forecasting with a Resource-Constrained Multi-Agent AI Framework" | Concise, punchy | Loses SRQ specificity |
| "From Descriptive to Predictive: A Multi-Agent AI Framework for FMCG Retail Demand Forecasting" | SRQ4 foregrounded | May overstate SRQ4 as the primary contribution |
| "AI-Augmented Demand Forecasting at the Edge: A Multi-Agent Framework for SME Retail" | Catchy, practical framing | "Edge" may mislead (≤8GB RAM, not IoT edge) |

**Recommendation**: use version 1 for submission; version 2 for presentations.
