# Thesis Archetypes

## 1. Theory-Heavy Qualitative Thesis
**Signals**: interpretive paradigm, no dataset, heavy theoretical framing, discourse/narrative/case analysis  
**Typical structure**:
- Introduction
- Literature Review
- Theoretical / Conceptual Framework
- Methodology (philosophy, approach, strategy, quality criteria)
- Analysis / Interpretation
- Discussion
- Conclusion

**Notes**: Theory chapter often separate from literature review. Methodology is philosophical, not technical.

---

## 2. Empirical Business / Social Science Thesis
**Signals**: primary data collection (interviews, surveys, observations), positivist or pragmatist, hypothesis or propositions  
**Typical structure**:
- Introduction
- Literature Review
- Conceptual Framework / Hypotheses
- Research Design and Methodology
- Results / Findings
- Discussion
- Conclusion (with implications and limitations)

**Notes**: Keep framework tight; do not duplicate concepts in Discussion.

---

## 3. Technical / AI / Engineering Thesis
**Signals**: system design, model training, algorithm development, performance evaluation, benchmarking  
**Typical structure**:
- Introduction
- Related Work
- Research Framework / Problem Formulation
- Methodology / System Design
- Implementation
- Evaluation / Results
- Discussion
- Conclusion

**Notes**: Implementation and Evaluation often deserve separate chapters. Related Work ≠ Literature Review in scope.

---

## 4. Design Science Research (DSR) Thesis
**Signals**: artefact development, iterations, demonstration, design cycles, IS/IT/MIS context  
**Typical structure**:
- Introduction
- Literature Review
- Theoretical Foundation (DSR methodology)
- Research Design
- Artefact Design and Development
- Demonstration
- Evaluation
- Discussion
- Conclusion

**Notes**: Artefact, Demonstration, Evaluation are distinct — do not collapse them unless iterations are simple.

---

## 5. Implementation-and-Evaluation Thesis
**Signals**: builds something (prototype, tool, system), then evaluates it, lighter on theory  
**Typical structure**:
- Introduction
- Background and Related Work
- Requirements and Design
- Implementation
- Evaluation
- Discussion
- Conclusion

**Notes**: Simpler structure is appropriate. Do not pad with unnecessary theory chapters.

---

## 6. Conceptual + Empirical Hybrid Thesis
**Signals**: develops a framework or model, then tests or illustrates it with data or cases  
**Typical structure**:
- Introduction
- Literature Review
- Conceptual Framework Development
- Methodology
- Empirical Study / Case Analysis
- Findings
- Discussion (framework validation or refinement)
- Conclusion

**Notes**: Framework development is a chapter, not buried in literature review.

---

## Archetype Selection Heuristic

Ask in order:
1. Is there a novel artefact or system being built? → DSR or Technical
2. Is there primary data collection? → Empirical Business or Hybrid
3. Is the contribution primarily theoretical or conceptual? → Theory-Heavy or Hybrid
4. Is the contribution a working implementation with evaluation? → Implementation-and-Evaluation
5. Does the thesis combine framework development with empirical illustration? → Hybrid
